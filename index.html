History

The Birth of the Sound

Charles Plate and John Bellamy founded Santa Cruz Sound Company in 1971. Together, they designed and built many custom speaker systems known for their durability and superb sound. Over forty years later, we remain in Santa Cruz going strong as a family business and providing quality sound systems for all your audio needs. In the spirit of Chuck and John, we will always tell it like it is, and if we don't have an answer, we will try to find it for our customers.
